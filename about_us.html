About us

Connecting Communities Through Local Events

EVENT ተራ (Event Tera) is a platform dedicated to centralizing and aggregating fragmented information about local, free community events in Ethiopia.

Our Mission

To significantly boost social engagement among young adults and students by making local events easily discoverable and accessible to everyone.

Our Community

We serve young adults, students, and anyone interested in discovering free cultural gatherings, workshops, and social activities across Ethiopian cities.

Our Values

We believe in free access to community events, cultural preservation, and connections that strengthen local communities.

Our Story

        EVENT ተራ was born from a simple observation:
        amazing community events were happening all around 
        Ethiopian cities, but information about them was
        scattered across social media posts, 
        word of mouth, and various platforms.

        Young adults and students were missing out on incredible
        opportunities to connect, learn, and engage with their
        communities simply because they didn't know these events
        existed.

        We created EVENT ተራ to solve this problem by building a 
        centralized platform where anyone can discover free local 
        events - from poetry nights and cultural performances to 
        tech meetups and community workshops.

        Our platform is designed to be simple, accessible, and focused
        on what matters: helping people find and attend events that 
        enrich their lives and strengthen community bonds.
